# Revenue & Compliance Use Cases Enabled

1. **Quote Validation in CPQ**
   - Validate proposed discounts against contractual floors/ceilings
   - Flag exceptions vs standard terms in real-time

2. **Rebate & Chargeback Alignment**
   - Retrieve applicable rebate schedules
   - Compare invoiced vs contracted pricing

3. **Account 360 for Key Customers**
   - Summarize key terms across all contracts for an account
   - Provide sales and finance with a contract-aware "snapshot"

4. **Revenue Forecasting Inputs**
   - Feed known price protections, volume tiers, and rebates into
     forecasting models

5. **Compliance Reporting**
   - Surface contracts with missing or non-standard clauses
   - Enable internal audit teams to search and analyze obligations quickly
