1. **Contract Authoring & Storage (CLM)**
   - Executed contracts
   - Amendments / addenda
   - Pricing & rebate schedules

2. **Integration & API Gateway**
   - Connectors from CLM/Repositories
   - Normalized contract service (REST/GraphQL)
   - Authentication, throttling, logging

3. **Contract Intelligence Layer**
   - Document parsing & chunking
   - Embeddings & vector database
   - RAG orchestration
   - Clause & risk extraction

4. **AI Services / Agents**
   - Q&A ("What are rebate terms for X?")
   - Comparison ("How does this differ from standard?")
   - Summarization & risk tagging

5. **Downstream Revenue & Compliance Systems**
   - CPQ / CRM (quote validation, deal guidance)
   - ERP (billing / invoicing alignment)
   - Analytics / dashboards (revenue, exposure, compliance)
